Variables in JavaScript

You know all about variables from math class. They are similar in JavaScript, but not exactly the same. In JavaScript the = sign means "assignment", not "equal". You have to "declare" and "assign" variables before you use them. Some programmers like to declare and assign their variables at the top of their program so they are easy to find.

We can use the keyword let to declare our variables, and the operator = to assign them.
